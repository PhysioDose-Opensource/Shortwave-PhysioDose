# PhysioDose: Shortwave Diathermy Dose Calculator

**Offline-ready Shortwave Diathermy (SWD) dose calculator (Single Page Application) for clinical, academic, and scientific use to standardize dosimetric reporting in physical therapy.**

## 📖 Description
Despite the widespread clinical use of Shortwave Diathermy (SWD)—both in pulsed (pSWD) and continuous (CSWD) modes—a recurrent and significant methodological flaw in physical therapy literature is the incomplete reporting of technical parameters and the omission of precise dosimetric calculations. This critical lack of standardization hinders the reproducibility of interventions and obscures the dose-response relationship in musculoskeletal treatments.

*PhysioDose* was developed to address this critical gap. This standalone tool provides researchers and clinicians with a rigorous, evidence-based calculator to compute the exact net thermal load delivered during SWD treatments. By inputting standard machine parameters (Peak Power, Pulse Width, Pulse Repetition Frequency, and Treatment Duration), the software accurately calculates the Duty Cycle, Mean Power (W), and Total Energy (kJ).

## ✨ Key Features
* **Zero-Installation & Offline-Ready:** Pure HTML/JS/CSS architecture ensuring immediate usability and long-term accessibility.
* **100% Patient Data Privacy:** No data is stored or transmitted to external servers.
* **Bilingual Interface:** Dynamic toggle between English and Spanish (EN/ES) for global accessibility.
* **Clinical Documentation:** Dedicated "Print / Save PDF" feature generating a formatted medical printout for electronic health records.
* **Core Dosimetry:** Calculation of Duty Cycle, Mean Power, and Total Delivered Energy for both Pulsed (pSWD) and Continuous (CSWD) modes.
* **Clinical Interpretation:** Evidence-based clinical interpretation of dosage based on current literature (Laufer et al., 2012; Masiero et al., 2019).
